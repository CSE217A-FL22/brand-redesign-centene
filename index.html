Our Story

Since its founding as a single local healthcare plan in 1984, Centene's heart and soul has been linked to the health of the communities we serve.
From that day forward, we have strived to provide exceptional care for our members by:

  • Focusing on the individual
  • Improving the whole health of a patient (not just at the doctor's office)
  • Supporting the community

Change starts with us.
As the leading managed care provider in the world,
we believe that we are in a unique position to create
a positive impact on the health of the world.

By leveraging our global infrastructure of doctors,
caretakers, and thought leaders we generate change from where it starts: the community.
